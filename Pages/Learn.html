About DIME

Founded by Master Esthetician Baylee Relf and her husband Ryan in
2018, DIME is a clean, EWG VERIFIED® skin wellness brand that offers
elevated solutions that deliver real results.

While studying at the National Institute of Medical Aesthetics, Baylee
learned skincare is not “one size fits all”. Her solution? Create a company
that provides optimized skincare for people of all ages.

With an ingredient-first approach to skin
wellness, DIME offers four skin-conscious product categories: skincare, body care, fragrance, and
beauty & wellness.

"I want customers to master their skin wellness journey with
DIME’s elevated, effective products. I am inviting you to get to
know and Love the DIME Difference.”
